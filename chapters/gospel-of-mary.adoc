== The Gospel of Mary

****
_The Gospel of Mary survives in fragmentary form — pages 1–6 and 11–14 are missing from the sole Coptic manuscript. What remains presents Mary Magdalene as the disciple who received teaching the others did not get. She describes a vision of the soul’s ascent past the powers that bind it to the material world.

Peter challenges her: “Did he really speak with a woman without our knowledge?” Levi rebukes Peter: “If the Saviour made her worthy, who are you to reject her?”

The missing pages almost certainly contained the core of Jesus’s private teaching to Mary. Their destruction — whether by time or by deliberate suppression — is one of the great losses of early Christian literature._

_Translation by Mark M. Mattison — gospels.net, public domain._
