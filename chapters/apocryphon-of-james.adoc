== The Apocryphon of James

****
_The Apocryphon (Secret Book) of James presents itself as a secret teaching given by Jesus to James and Peter after the resurrection — but before the ascension. It was discovered at Nag Hammadi in 1945.

In this text, Jesus gives extended teaching about the Kingdom, suffering, and the nature of faith. When the other disciples ask to hear, Jesus takes only James and Peter aside. The teaching emphasises that the Kingdom requires active seeking: “Be eager to be saved without being urged. Rather, be fervent on your own and, if possible, surpass even me.”

James — Jesus’s brother — is presented as the primary recipient of this secret teaching, not Peter. This aligns with the historical evidence that James, not Peter, led the Jerusalem community._

_Translation by Mark M. Mattison — gospels.net, public domain._
